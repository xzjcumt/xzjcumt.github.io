In a hazy and half awake condition, Rick saw his good friend Shane visiting him in the hospital. As he wanted to talk to Shane, he found that he couldn't find him. After he came into consciousness, he found the hospital empty. He realized that there must be something big outside. But he didn't know what happened on earth. He stumbled out of the hospital, then he found the corpses everywhere...
The Walking Dead is a kind of horror, and its scene is large, which makes the characters trapped step by step. It focus on the depiction of human nature. The story of the show is mainly to explore the human nature. Its screenplay writers are Frank Darabont and Robert Kirkman. On October 31, 2010, by director Ernest R. Dickerson, The Walking Dead began officially released in the United States.
Hero Rick Grimes (Andrew Lincoln) was the deputy sheriff of a small town in Atlanta suburb. In the process of performing official duties, Rick was shot and seriously wounded, then he was taken to a local hospital for emergency treatment. During his coma, the outside world was attacked by zombies. After Rick woke up, he quickly returned home to find his wife Lori (Sarah Wayne Callies) and son Carl. However, they and their neighbors were all missing. Rick was under the zombie' attack, a black father and his son (Morgan and Duane) hiding in the neighbor's house saved him. They told Rick the truth of "zombie plague", and also warned him: If he wants to survive in the world of zombies, he couldn't be bitten by them, even be scratched. Otherwise, he would be virus infection, death, then become a member of the "zombie army". The father and his son also explained the several types of zombies. The most common were "walkers" -- they were walking slowly, just shooting them in the head could kill them thoroughly. Although Rick had a gun in his hand, there were so many "walkers" outside. The two also revealed the important information: Rick' family may have been to "temporary safety zone" which the government set up in Atlanta. With a glimmer of hope, Rick decided to go to Atlanta to find his family...At the end of the first part, Rick rode over to Atlantic city, only to find that the city was filled with zombie. Surrounded by zombies, Rick helplessly went into a tank, the zombies surrounded the tank layer upon layer. All of a sudden, the radio in the tank rang: Hay! The guy in the tank！Are you cozy there！

The Walking Dead is a good zombie drama. Instead of being a variation of undead zombies in Resident Evil, in The Walking Dead they are civilians. The threat comes not only from the zombies, but also from the contradictions between human, which can reflect the current social reality. The show portrays the character's psychological activity and the change, and the dark side of human nature is revealed in the catastrophe... In the story, the protagonist has been thrown into despair again and again, meanwhile, it also gives the viewer a disappointment just like the end of the world.
